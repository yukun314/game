Chip Remover - Privacy Policy

Welcome to Chip Remover. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using our application.

Information Collection

Chip Remover is an offline puzzle game, and we do not actively collect any personal identification information. Our application does not require users to register an account, nor do we ask users to provide personal information.

Automatically Collected Information

Our application may collect certain non-personal identification information, including but not limited to:

  • Device information: such as device model, operating system version, device identifiers, etc.
  • Game data: such as game progress, game settings, etc.
  • Performance data: such as application crash reports, performance metrics, etc.

This information is used solely for improving application performance and user experience and will not be used to identify specific users.

Third-Party Services

Our application may use third-party services such as Google Play Services and Google Analytics. These services may collect information and follow their own privacy policies. We recommend that you review the privacy policies of these third-party services for more information.

Data Storage

Game data is primarily stored locally on your device. We do not upload your game data to cloud servers.

Children's Privacy

Our application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we update the Privacy Policy, we will notify you through the application or by other means. We recommend that you review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
